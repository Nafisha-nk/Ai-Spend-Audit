## Interview Script

1. What AI tools does your team pay for today?
2. Who decides whether a new AI tool gets purchased?
3. When was the last time you reviewed AI spend?
4. What would make you trust or distrust an automated savings recommendation?
5. If a tool found $500/month in credible savings, what would you do next?
