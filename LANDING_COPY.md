## FAQ

### Do I need to create an account?

No. The audit runs without login, and email is only requested after you see the result.

### Is this just recommending the cheapest tool?

No. The engine checks plan fit, seat count, overlapping tools, use case, and whether spend is large enough for discounted credits.

### What happens if my stack is already efficient?

The report says so. Low-savings audits are treated as good news, not forced into fake recommendations.

### Does the public report show my email or company?

No. Public reports show tools, savings, and recommendations, but lead-identifying fields are stored separately.

### Why does Credex appear in some reports?

Credex is highlighted only when savings are large enough that discounted AI infrastructure credits may be more valuable than basic plan changes.
